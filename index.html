The Friends of the Mole Hash House Harriers - fotmh3 - running club welcomes runners of all abilities to join us at any of our cross county Family Fun Runs. We organise bi-monthly Runs in east Kent from a Pub on Sunday morning at 11:00. Dates are usually the second and the last Sunday in the month. We always arrange for a meal after the Run. We are a Dog friendly group and try arrange dog friendly Pubs.
